Refund Policy

14-Day Risk-Free Guarantee. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Last updated: February 24, 2026

✅

100% Risk-Free Guarantee

We are so confident you'll love Smart Load Solution that we offer a complete 14-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked.

If for any reason you are not satisfied with your subscription, you can request a full refund within the first 14 days from your first payment. No explanations needed.

👤

1. Who Is Eligible?

✅ You qualify for a refund if:

  • You are within the first 14 days from your first payment
  • You have a Professional ($14.99/mo) or Premium + AI ($29.99/mo) subscription
  • It is your first refund request

❌ Refunds do not apply if:

  • More than 14 days have passed since your first payment
  • A previous refund was already processed on your account
  • Your account was suspended for violating the Terms of Use
📝

2. How to Request a Refund

Just follow these simple steps:

Step 1

Send an email to support@smartloadsolution.com with the subject "Refund Request"

Step 2

Include your registration email so we can locate your account quickly

Step 3

You will receive a confirmation of your request within 24 hours

Done!

The refund will be processed within 3-5 business days to your original payment method

⏰

3. Processing Times

Stage Time
Request confirmation Within 24 hours
Refund processing 3-5 business days
Reflection in your statement 5-10 business days (depends on your bank)
🔄

4. What Happens After the Refund?

  • Your subscription will be cancelled immediately
  • Your account will move to the Starter plan (free)
  • Your data will be kept for 30 days in case you decide to return
  • You can resubscribe at any time
ℹ️

5. Cancellation vs Refund

❌ Cancellation

You stop future charges. You keep access until the end of the paid period. No refund of remaining time.

💰 Refund

We return 100% of your payment. Your subscription is cancelled immediately. Only available in the first 14 days.
