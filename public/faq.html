Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SmartLoad Solution.

General

What is SmartLoad Solution?

SmartLoad Solution is a professional platform designed for cargo van expediters. It lets you calculate the real RPM of each load, identify profitable zones, track your expenses, and make data-driven financial decisions — all from your phone or computer.

Do I need technical experience to use it?

No. SmartLoad is designed for expediters, not engineers. The interface is intuitive and you can calculate your first load in under 5 minutes.

Does it work on iOS and Android?

Yes. SmartLoad is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on any device with a modern browser. You can install it on your phone directly from the browser, no App Store or Google Play required.

Plans & Pricing

Is there a free version?

Yes. The Starter plan is completely free and includes the RPM calculator, basic profitability analysis, and load tracker. No credit card required.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes. No contracts or long-term commitments. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings and will retain access until the end of your paid period.

What's the difference between Professional and Premium?

Professional includes modules 1–6 with advanced financial analysis. Premium includes all modules (1–8) plus full access to Lex AI, the smart assistant that analyzes your loads in real time. See the plans page for a detailed comparison.

Account & Data

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use Google's Firebase for data storage with encryption in transit and at rest. We never sell your information to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I access from multiple devices?

Yes. Your account syncs in real time across all your devices. Calculate from your phone on the road and review your finances from your computer at home.
