Costos Visibles vs Costos Invisibles

📖 7 min lectura 🎓 Módulo 1 - Lección 2

🔍 EL PROBLEMA

La mayoría solo piensa en:

  • ✅ Gasolina
  • ✅ Tolls (cuando los hay)
❌ ERROR GRAVE

Hay costos invisibles que están comiendo tus ganancias en silencio. Los costos visibles representan solo el 60-70% de tus costos totales.

📊 TABLA DE COSTOS REALES

Straight Truck / Sprinter Van (por milla)

COSTOS VARIABLES Rango
Combustible $0.42 - $0.50
DEF (Diesel Exhaust Fluid) $0.03 - $0.05
Mantenimiento programado $0.08 - $0.12
Desgaste de llantas $0.03 - $0.04
Tolls/Parking (promedio) $0.02 - $0.05
SUBTOTAL VARIABLE $0.58 - $0.76
COSTOS FIJOS Rango

TOTAL COSTO OPERATIVO: $0.88 - $1.25 /milla

* Basado en 100,000 millas anuales

👻 LOS COSTOS INVISIBLES MÁS IMPORTANTES

1. DEPRECIACIÓN ($0.05-$0.08/milla)

El más "invisible" pero muy real:

  • Compra de van: $80,000
  • Valor en 5 años: $30,000
  • Pérdida de valor: $50,000
  • = $0.10/milla de pérdida

No ves esta salida de dinero diariamente, pero cuando vendas tu van, sentirás el impacto.

2. PAGO DE VAN ($0.15-$0.25/milla)

⚠️ OJO: Si haces MENOS millas, este costo POR MILLA sube:
  • 6,000 millas/mes: $1,800 ÷ 6,000 = $0.30/milla
  • 10,000 millas/mes: $1,800 ÷ 10,000 = $0.18/milla

Tu costo fijo por milla mejora haciendo MÁS millas (si mantienes la rentabilidad).

3. SEGURO ($0.08-$0.12/milla)

Seguro anual típico:

  • Liability: $6,000
  • Physical damage: $3,000
  • Cargo: $1,500
  • TOTAL: $10,500/año = $0.105/milla

Pagas esto AUNQUE NO RUEDES. Por eso necesitas maximizar tus millas rentables.

🚨 LA REGLA DE LOS COSTOS INVISIBLES

Si tu van está RODANDO, está generando costos.
Si tu van está PARADA, algunos costos siguen corriendo.

NO existe "día libre" para: Seguro, Pago de van, Depreciación, Licencias

🚨 RED FLAGS DE COSTOS

Señales de que tus costos están fuera de control:
  • ⚠️ Combustible >$0.55/milla → Revisa driving style
  • ⚠️ Mantenimiento >$0.15/milla → Tu van necesita atención NOW
  • ⚠️ Costos fijos >$0.50/milla → Necesitas más millas o reducir pagos
  • ⚠️ Costo total >$1.30/milla → Difícil ser rentable, revisa operación

💡 CONCLUSIÓN CLAVE

Los costos invisibles son REALES.

Solo porque no los ves cada día no significa que no existan.

Tu van SIEMPRE cuesta dinero.
La pregunta es: ¿estás ganando MÁS de lo que cuesta?

Visible Costs vs Hidden Costs

📖 7 min read 🎓 Module 1 - Lesson 2

🔍 THE PROBLEM

Most operators only think about:

  • ✅ Fuel
  • ✅ Tolls (when applicable)
❌ SERIOUS MISTAKE

There are hidden costs silently eating your profits. Visible costs represent only 60-70% of your total operating costs.

📊 REAL COST TABLE

Straight Truck / Sprinter Van (per mile)

VARIABLE COSTS Range
Fuel $0.42 - $0.50
DEF (Diesel Exhaust Fluid) $0.03 - $0.05
Scheduled maintenance $0.08 - $0.12
Tire wear $0.03 - $0.04
Tolls/Parking (average) $0.02 - $0.05
VARIABLE SUBTOTAL $0.58 - $0.76
FIXED COSTS Range
Van payment/lease $0.15 - $0.25
Insurance (liability + cargo) $0.08 - $0.12
Annual licenses/permits $0.01 - $0.02
Vehicle depreciation $0.05 - $0.08
Phone/apps/technology $0.01 - $0.02
FIXED SUBTOTAL $0.30 - $0.49
TOTAL OPERATING COST: $0.88 - $1.25 /mile

* Based on 100,000 miles per year

👻 THE MOST IMPORTANT HIDDEN COSTS

1. DEPRECIATION ($0.05-$0.08/mile)

The most "invisible" — but very real:

  • Van purchase: $80,000
  • Value in 5 years: $30,000
  • Loss in value: $50,000
  • = $0.10/mile in losses

You don't see this money leave daily, but when you sell your van, you'll feel the impact.

2. VAN PAYMENT ($0.15-$0.25/mile)

⚠️ HEADS UP: If you run FEWER miles, this cost PER MILE goes up:
  • 6,000 miles/month: $1,800 ÷ 6,000 = $0.30/mile
  • 10,000 miles/month: $1,800 ÷ 10,000 = $0.18/mile

Your fixed cost per mile improves by running MORE miles (as long as you stay profitable).

3. INSURANCE ($0.08-$0.12/mile)

Typical annual insurance:

  • Liability: $6,000
  • Physical damage: $3,000
  • Cargo: $1,500
  • TOTAL: $10,500/year = $0.105/mile

You pay this EVEN WHEN YOU'RE NOT MOVING. That's why you need to maximize profitable miles.

🚨 THE HIDDEN COST RULE

When your van is MOVING, it's generating costs.
When your van is PARKED, some costs keep running.

There is NO "day off" for: Insurance, Van payment, Depreciation, Licenses

🚨 COST RED FLAGS

Signs your costs are out of control:
  • ⚠️ Fuel >$0.55/mile → Review your driving style
  • ⚠️ Maintenance >$0.15/mile → Your van needs attention NOW
  • ⚠️ Fixed costs >$0.50/mile → You need more miles or lower payments
  • ⚠️ Total cost >$1.30/mile → Hard to be profitable — review your operation

💡 KEY TAKEAWAY

Hidden costs are REAL.

Just because you don't see them every day doesn't mean they don't exist.

Your van ALWAYS costs money.
The question is: are you earning MORE than it costs?
