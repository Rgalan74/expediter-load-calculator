Errores Comunes al Introducir Datos (Y Cómo Evitarlos)

📖 8 min lectura 🎓 Módulo 2 - Lección 4

🚨 LOS 7 ERRORES MÁS COSTOSOS

❌ ERROR #1: Ignorar Repositioning

El Error: Dejar campo "Repositioning" vacío cuando delivery es en zona muerta.

La Realidad: Load en Laredo requiere 280 millas a San Antonio.
RPM mostrado: $1.60/mi → RPM REAL: $1.25/mi
Diferencia: $0.35/mi de error = $448 de "ganancia fantasma"

✅ La Solución: SIEMPRE pregúntate "¿Hay freight donde entrego?" Si NO → Ingresar repositioning.

❌ ERROR #2: Usar Direcciones Incompletas

El Error: Ingresar solo "Chicago" (ciudad es enorme: 100+ millas)

La Realidad: "Chicago" puede ser Downtown, O'Hare (15mi), Joliet (40mi), Aurora (45mi).
Error potencial: 40-80 millas

✅ La Solución:
❌ Malo: "Chicago"
✅ Mejor: "Chicago, IL"
✅✅ BEST: "1234 Main St, Chicago, IL 60601"

❌ ERROR #3: No Actualizar Current Location

El Error: Lunes estabas en Detroit, martes viajaste a Atlanta, pero app sigue mostrando Detroit.

La Realidad: Deadhead calculation está COMPLETAMENTE wrong.

✅ La Solución: Cada vez que abres calculator, verify location. Enable GPS auto-update en Settings.

❌ ERROR #4: Olvidar Tolls y Fees en Payment

El Error: Broker: "$1,200 base + $60 tolls" → Usuario ingresa: $1,200 ❌

La Realidad: Si broker reembolsa tolls, eso es REVENUE adicional.

✅ La Solución: Suma TODO:
Base: $1,200 + FSC: $150 + Tolls: $60 + Detention: $50 = $1,460 total

❌ ERROR #5: Confundir "Loaded Miles" con "Total Miles"

El Error: Broker dice "800 millas" → Usuario piensa "total" → Ingresa en campo incorrecto

La Realidad: Brokers casi SIEMPRE quotean LOADED miles only. Deadhead es tu responsabilidad.

✅ La Solución: 99% del tiempo, deja "Loaded Miles" en BLANCO. App calculará routing completo.

❌ ERROR #6: No Guardar Loads Rechazados

El Error: Calculas load → Malo → Cierras app (no hay record)

La Realidad: Lex AI no aprende, no tienes reference para loads similares futuros.

✅ La Solución: SIEMPRE save en History, aunque sea DECLINE. Lex aprende tus preferencias.

❌ ERROR #7: Usar CPM Incorrecto o Desactualizado

El Error: CPM configurado hace 6 meses: $0.95/mi. Fuel subió, seguro aumentó → CPM real: $1.15/mi

La Realidad: App muestra loads como "profitable" cuando NO lo son.

✅ La Solución: Revisa tu CPM MENSUALMENTE. Settings → Cost Per Mile → [Update]

Common Data Entry Mistakes (And How to Uncomplicate Them)

📖 8 min read 🎓 Module 2 - Lesson 4

🚨 THE 7 MOST EXPENSIVE MISTAKES

❌ MISTAKE #1: Ignoring Repositioning Miles

The Mistake: Leaving the "Repositioning" field completely blank when you deliver to a dead zone.

The Reality: Say a load goes to Laredo, TX. You WILL need to drive 280 empty miles back to San Antonio just to find the next load.
Apparent RPM: $1.60/mile → REAL RPM: $1.25/mile
The impact: A $0.35/mile error = roughly $448 in "phantom profits" that don't exist.

✅ The Fix: ALWAYS ask yourself: "Is there freight exactly where I'm delivering?" If the answer is NO → Add repositioning miles. Simple as that.

❌ MISTAKE #2: Using Incomplete Vague Addresses

The Mistake: Typing just "Chicago" (the metropolitan area is massive, easily spanning over 100 miles).

The Reality: "Chicago" could mean Downtown, the O'Hare airport (15mi away), Joliet (40mi away), or Aurora (45mi away).
The impact: A potential error of 40-80 miles.

✅ The Fix:
❌ Bad: "Chicago"
✅ Better: "Chicago, IL"
✅✅ BEST: "1234 Main St, Chicago, IL 60601"

❌ MISTAKE #3: Forgetting to Update Current Location

The Mistake: You were in Detroit on Monday, drove to Atlanta on Tuesday, but your calculator still thinks you're in Detroit.

The Reality: Your entire deadhead calculation will be COMPLETELY wrong.

✅ The Fix: Every single time you open the calculator, verify your location. We highly recommend enabling GPS auto-update in your Settings to take that off your plate.

❌ MISTAKE #4: Leaving Tolls and Fees out of Payment

The Mistake: The broker says: "$1,200 base + $60 tolls" → You enter: "$1,200". ❌

The Reality: If the broker is reimbursing tolls or detentions, that is additional REVENUE hitting your account.

✅ The Fix: Add EVERYTHING together:
Base: $1,200 + FSC: $150 + Tolls: $60 + Detention: $50 = $1,460 total income

❌ MISTAKE #5: Confusing "Loaded Miles" with "Total Miles"

The Mistake: Broker says "it's an 800-mile run" → You assume that's total miles → You input it incorrectly.

The Reality: Brokers almost ALWAYS quote LOADED miles only. Your deadhead to the pickup is completely your problem.

✅ The Fix: 99% of the time, just leave the "Loaded Miles" field BLANK. Let the app automatically calculate the exact routing for you.

❌ MISTAKE #6: Failing to Save Rejected Loads

The Mistake: You calculate a load → It looks terrible → You close the app (leaving no record that it ever happened).

The Reality: Our Lex AI doesn't learn, and you lose valuable reference data for similar loads in the future.

✅ The Fix: ALWAYS hit save to History, even if you DECLINED it. Lex will quickly learn your preferences and start pointing out trends.

❌ MISTAKE #7: Using an Outdated Cost Per Mile (CPM)

The Mistake: You configured your CPM 6 months ago at $0.95/mile. Meanwhile, fuel skyrocketed and insurance went up → Your actual CPM is now $1.15/mile.

The Reality: The app will mistakenly tell you loads are "profitable" when you're actually losing cash.

✅ The Fix: Review your CPM MONTHLY. Just head to Settings → Cost Per Mile → Hit [Update]. Stay sharp.

📋 THE CHECKLIST: Before Calculating ANY Load

✅ THE PRE-CALCULATION CHECKLIST

  • ☐ Is my current location correct (or auto-updated)?
  • ☐ Do I have the FULL pickup address (not just a city)?
  • ☐ Do I have the FULL delivery address?
  • ☐ Did I include ALL payment details (base + FSC + tolls + fees)?
  • ☐ Do I know if the delivery zone will require repositioning?
  • ☐ Is my CPM accurately updated for this month?
  • ☐ Did I clarify if the broker quote was "loaded" or "total" miles?

If every single box gets a ✅ → Run the calculation with absolute confidence.
If you're missing info → Call the broker immediately and get it.
