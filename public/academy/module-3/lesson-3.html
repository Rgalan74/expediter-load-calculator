🎯 PRO TRICKS TO MASTER THE HEAT MAP

1. The 15-Minute Weekly Planning Session

Every Sunday, dedicate 15 minutes to planning. This small habit translates to roughly +$200-$400 more per week.

  • Open up your Heat Map.
  • Check out your current location.
  • Identify 3 target "Dark Green" zones for the week.
  • Set alerts specifically for loads heading to those zones.
  • Track your performance against your plan.

2. The Seasonal Master Adjustment

Right before the start of every quarter (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4):

  • Change your view to "Last 3 months."
  • Compare it with the exact same quarter from last year.
  • Adjust your driving circuit based on emerging patterns.
  • Update your primary target zones.

For Example:
Q4 → Add more Florida routes (the snowbirds are flocking south)
Q2 → Heavily reduce Phoenix (summer is totally dead)

3. The Broker Overlay Trick

Combine your Heat Map with your Broker Performance metrics:

"Show me zones where Broker John is highly active."

Strategy: When you're sitting in Detroit or Chicago, you prioritize John's offers first.
