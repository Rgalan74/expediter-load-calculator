🔮 CHAIN THINKING: THE 4 REQUIRED QUESTIONS

Before you accept absolutely ANY load, ask yourself:

  1. Exactly where does this drop me off?
    What is the Zone Score? What is the expected wait time?
  2. What are my realistic options from there?
    Are there multiple high-quality outbound lanes? Or am I effectively trapped?
  3. Does this drive me TOWARDS my home circuit or PULL ME AWAY from it?
    Drawing me into profitability? Or pulling me toward a desert?
  4. What is my honest 3-load chain projection?
    Total net profit? Daily average profit?

If 4 answers are positive → Accept instantly.
If 3/4 are positive → Probably accept.
If 2/4 are positive → Negotiate fiercely or pause to analyze.
If ≤ 1 is positive → Automatically decline.
