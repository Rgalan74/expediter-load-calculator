❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a credit card for the free plan?

+
No. The free plan gives you 50 loads without needing to enter any payment method. Just create your account and start using it immediately.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

+
Yes, you can cancel anytime from your settings panel. No contracts or penalties. Once cancelled, you keep access until the end of the already paid period.

Is my data safe?

+
Absolutely. We use Firebase (Google Cloud) for storage, with end-to-end encryption. Your financial data is private and we never share it with third parties.

Does it work on my phone?

+
Yes. Smart Load Solution is a 100% responsive Progressive Web App (PWA). It works on any device: iPhone, Android, tablet, or desktop computer.

What happens if I exceed the 50 free loads?

+
You'll receive a notification when you approach the limit. You'll still be able to view your history and access the dashboard, but you'll need to upgrade to the Pro Plan to add more loads.

Can I export my data to Excel?

+
Yes. Pro Plan users have unlimited export to Excel and CSV from any section (history, finances, zones).
